Frequently Asked

Why use PHP instead of Node.js? ↓

Modern PHP (8.3) is exceptionally fast and has more mature libraries for stable business logic and database management. For enterprise CRUD apps and e-commerce, it's often the superior choice for reliability and deployment ease.

How does AI factor into your development? ↓

I use AI to handle the "grunt work"—unit testing, boilerplate generation, and initial refactoring. This allows me to focus 100% on the architecture and high-level logic, delivering weeks of work in days.

Do you work with existing MySQL databases? ↓

Yes. I specialize in taking legacy, slow databases and optimizing them via indexing, normalization, and query refactoring without data loss.
